What We Do

Shri knitwear supplies a wide range of knit garments in various style, designs and colours We have a range of quality products for Men’s, Women’s, Childrens and Infants from "Basics to High Fashion" We work with all kinds of knit fabric materials such as 100% Cotton, Cotton/ Elastane, Polyester, Cotton /Polyester, Viscose, Modal, Organic Cotton, 100% Polyester and other blended knitted fabrics, Also we supply products in organic Cotton, Soyo and Bamboo

About Us

Shri Knitwear is a reputable and fast growing knitwear company based in Tirupur, India. Shri Knitwear was established in the year 2010 with a dedicated team of highly experienced and professional people from the knitwear manufacturing industry. We engage in manufacturing and Exporting knitted garments for various retail brands and chain stores across the globe. We offer our clients with products of impeccable quality, on-time delivery, competitive price, reliable and committed service. Our products ranges from basics to high fashion garments targeting knits segment for Men’s, Women’s, Kid’s and Babies categories. Our manufacturing facility is vertically integrated with state of Art machineries to deliver high quality products and we strictly follow highest ethical and environmental standards in our manufacturing. Shri Knitwear works on the principles of "Win - Win" strategy for mutual growth& long term relationship with our buyers, vendors and employees.
